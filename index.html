Features

Hand-drawn. Really

Every icon had been lovingly doodled, which can not be substituted by any filters or effects. Jolly Icons will add authenticity and personal touch to your project.

Customizable

Jolly Icons are vectorized, which means unlimited scalability. You can easily change color or angle without any quality loss. You can use the icons with different resolutions, making them compatible with regular screens, retina displays and print media.

Diverse

The set contains the most essential icons for web sites and app interfaces, and many more. There are hand-drawn icons covering such topics as e-commerce, business, food, weather, lifestyle, science, health, medicine, media, games, social networks and even more!
